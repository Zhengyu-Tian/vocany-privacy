Vocany Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Vocany ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Vocany app.

2. Information We Collect

To provide app functionality, we collect the following information:

2.1 Account Information

  • Name: Collected through Apple Sign In for user authentication and account management
  • Email Address: Collected through Apple Sign In (if user authorizes) for account identification
  • User ID: Unique identifier generated by Supabase to identify your account

2.2 User Content

  • Learning Content: Words you input, translation requests, etc., used to provide AI translation and sentence generation features
  • Learning Data: Your learning progress, vocabulary lists, etc., stored locally on your device

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: User authentication, account management, providing AI translation services
  • Data Storage: Securely storing your account information on Supabase servers
  • Service Provision: Providing translation and sentence generation through Cloudflare Worker and ChatGPT API

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage
  • Cloudflare Worker: For API forwarding
  • OpenAI ChatGPT API: For providing AI translation and sentence generation features

These service providers have their own privacy policies, and we recommend you review them.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Using encrypted connections (HTTPS) for data transmission
  • Securely storing data on Supabase servers
  • Local learning data stored on your device

6. Data Retention

We retain your data in the following circumstances:

  • Account Information: Retained while you use the app, deleted after account deletion
  • Local Learning Data: Stored on your device, controlled by you

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent (by deleting account)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you in the app or through other means.
